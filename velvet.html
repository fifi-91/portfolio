The Velvet Room

A Cinematic Salon for Women Rewriting Their Stories

SOPHIA AKROFI • 2026

Building an intimate podcast platform and digital brand that centers Black women's narratives and the private turning points behind public identities.

Role Brand Strategist, Lead Product Designer

Client type B2C Media Client

CHALLENGE

Eugena Washington, a celebrated model, actress and cultural voice, envisioned a platform that moves beyond surface-level celebrity.

The Velvet Room needed to be more than a podcast—it needed to be a sanctuary where women could explore the moments that forced them to reinvent themselves.

The challenge: designing a digital experience that honors the intimacy of conversation while establishing a distinctive brand identity for a women-by-women platform in a saturated media landscape.

DESIGN APPROACH

We anchored the brand around three principles:

Cinematic Intimacy — Visual language that mirrors the salon aesthetic: luxe, curated, intentional.

Narrative-First Architecture — The digital experience needed to foreground the conversations themselves, not distract from them.

Cultural Specificity — Centering Black women’s voices meant design choices (typography, imagery, color) that reflected that identity authentically—not performatively.

Brand Development

Researching key themes that would make the digital experience feel like listeners were entering a divinely feminine world.

Logo development

The Velvet Room logo was developed using Eugena's distinctive handwriting.

Interface Design

Using Figma to design immersive digital experiences, with textured layers and themes close to Eugena’s experience.

Sovereign Storytelling

Creating a collaborative space in order to create the tale the client wished for

While working with Eugena, I came to understand her incredible career in fashion but most importantly, the path that shaped her.

She wanted to capture a visual representation of her path to date

Coding the website

Bringing The Velvet Room to life required translating a cinematic brand into a responsive, high-performance digital experience.

The site was built from scratch using HTML, CSS and JavaScript, ensuring full control over layout, motion and storytelling.
